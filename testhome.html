WELCOME TO RIVER'S EDGE


River’s Edge Reception and Hotel is not just another ordinary hotel situated in your town.
It’s where the old meets the new. It is built around a walawwa which homed about 4
generations and situated just next to the beautiful Madampa River just 1.5km from
the heart of Ambalangoda, a town well known for its ancient devil masks and
devil dancers. Also within reach of Colombo Airport (BIA), a 136km
drive along the Southern Highway would be the fastest to reach the destination.
